Update/Reset

Your TiltBridge can be updated over the internet to the latest version of the TiltBridge firmware by clicking the "Update Firmware" button below. Additionally, if you plan on relocating your TiltBridge to a new network, you can reset the WiFi connection settings by clicking "Reset WiFi".
